Who We Are

We are a non-profit, volunteer-driven community connecting Ukrainians everywhere and welcoming friends of Ukraine. We share culture and language, offer peer support for newcomers, and build lasting friendships.

Community & Friendship

Meet people nearby or abroad, find your circle, and stay connected through gatherings and online spaces.

Culture & Language

Celebrate traditions, practice Ukrainian and English, and explore our shared heritage through events and resources.

Support for Newcomers

Peer guidance on housing, paperwork, schools, and everyday life—by people who’ve been there.

Give Back

Volunteer your time or skills. Lead a meetup, mentor newcomers, or help organize cultural projects.
